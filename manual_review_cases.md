# Manual Review Cases

These 10 customers were selected for manual review because the data shows a mix of positive and negative signals. In cases like these, segmentation alone is not enough to decide the best retention action.

---

## Case 1 — CUST00006

**Segment:** High-Value Unhappy | **RFM:** R=3, F=4, M=4 | **Score:** 11  
**Recency:** 51 days | **Frequency:** 5 orders | **Monetary:** ₹3,770  
**Tickets:** 2 | **Avg Sentiment:** -0.68 | **Return Rate:** 20%  
**Sessions (30d):** 2 | **Campaign:** none | **Loyalty:** Silver | **Actual Churn:** 0
  
This customer placed 5 orders worth ₹3,770 and has not churned yet. The customer is still visiting the website, but there are 2 support tickets, a negative sentiment score (-0.68), and a 20% return rate. The purchase history is strong, but the support data suggests dissatisfaction.

**Suggested action:**  
Contact the customer and understand the reasons behind the tickets and returns. Focus on resolving the issue instead of offering discounts.
